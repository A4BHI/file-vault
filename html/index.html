Overview

Vaultx is a simple yet powerful platform to securely share files with end-to-end encryption. Whether you send via link, QR code, or email, your data stays visible only to you and your recipient.

🔐 End-to-End Encryption

Your files are encrypted directly in your browser before being uploaded.

🧩 Password & Expiry Links

Add passwords and expiry dates to your shared links for maximum control.
